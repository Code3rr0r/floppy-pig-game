## üéÆ How to Play

1. Press **SPACE**, **CLICK**, or **TAP** to make the pig flap
2. Navigate through the green pipes without hitting them
3. Each pipe passed earns you 1 point
4. Try to beat your high score!
5. Game gets faster as your score increases
